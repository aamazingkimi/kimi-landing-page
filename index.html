Frequently Asked Questions

What is the implementation timeline? +
Standard deployment takes 48 hours from infrastructure access. Enterprise deployments with custom integrations typically require 3-5 business days.
What infrastructure requirements are needed? +
Minimum: 4 vCPUs, 8GB RAM, 50GB storage on AWS (t3.xlarge), Azure (D4s v3), or GCP (n2-standard-4).
How is data privacy maintained? +
All data processing occurs within your cloud infrastructure. No data leaves your environment. We never access or store your data.
